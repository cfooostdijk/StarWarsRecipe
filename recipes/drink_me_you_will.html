Drink Me, You Will!

p>

Description

Bryan Gall of Portland, Ore.'s Bacchus Bar at Kimpton Hotel Vintage uses a bright bunch of smashed basil to give this Yoda-inspired drink its requisite gasp of green, a hue reminiscent of the Jedi master’s lovely skin tone. Sage simple syrup is a natural nod to Yoda’s wisdom, and a couple of those leaves make a nice, perky Yoda ear garnish too.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces Calvados
  • 1 ounce apple cider
  • 1/2 ounce sage simple syrup*
  • 1/2 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 6 basil leaves
  • Garnish: sage leaves

Steps

  1. Add the Calvados, apple cider, sage simple syrup, lemon juice and basil leaves to a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Double-strain into a Martini glass.
  3. Garnish with 2 fresh sage leaves. If possible, clip to either side of the glass using miniature clothespins or a pick.
