What could melt the slick, seemingly frosty demeanor of a stoic storm trooper? Well, for starters, Ryan Morris, the general manager of Venkmanâ€™s in Atlanta, thinks one must start with rum. Of course! Sometimes, when the weather calls for it in the balmy South, he likes to run this tropically tweaked tipple through a slushy machine. But you can shake it, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Bayou rum
  • 2 oz Coconut milk
  • 2 oz Pineapple juice
  • 2 oz Fresh orange juice

Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients into a shaker with ice and shake.
  2. Strain into a Collins glass over ice.
